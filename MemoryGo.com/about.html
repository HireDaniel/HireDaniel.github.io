Remember it once and forever... such a nice slogan , every student hears about , but only few so called memory athletes who charge an enourmous amount of money for books and courses and a lot of time for the training are able to use these special techniques.

The idea behind Memory Go is to enable everyone to use the techniques of the memory masters to level up their memory game , wheather it's for academic purposes, for work or to expand your personal interests and capabilites far beyond your imagination.

Consistently used, these simple webpage will make your studies way easier. Instead of remembering a boring something - experience the images, locations and stories provided for you.

As a former struggling student and a low income onfailing entrepreneur i saw the benefits of being able to memorize every topic with ease, of course you need to understand it first, but the more you know the easier it is to understand a new topic.

The main problem is that most people just aren't able to put in the time for an effective memory routine and its hard to create creative images of your own and build entire memory palaces from scratch while struggling to understand a given topic.

No more!

Memory Go started as help for myself, but has expanded into a growing community full of loving and supporting people all around the world.
